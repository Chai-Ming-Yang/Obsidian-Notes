**PCI DSS** *Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard*
- security of payment card transactions

**HIPPA** *Health Insurance Portability & Accountability Act*
1. protect health insurance coverage (upon job change / lay-off)
2. safe electronic access to patients' health data
3. tax-related provision and medical savings guidelines
4. expand health insurance reforms (pre-existing conditions) (need continued coverage)
5. rules for company-owned life insurances

**GDPR** *General Data Protection Regulation*
- enhanced control over data privacy (Europeans)

**PIPEDA** *Personal Information Protection & Electronic Documents Act*
- regulation usage & disclosure of client's personal info
	- consent needed *(review accuracy of info)*
